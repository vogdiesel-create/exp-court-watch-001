# CourtWatch

**Litigation intelligence for investors. Know about lawsuits before the market reacts.**

## The Problem

Material litigation events move stock prices but most investors learn about them from news articles days after filing. Federal court dockets (PACER) are expensive and tedious to search manually.

## The Solution

CourtWatch monitors federal court filings for cases involving public companies, classifies them by potential material impact, and alerts investors before the information is widely known.

## Features

- Federal court filing monitoring (all districts)
- Public company entity matching
- Material impact classification
- Historical case outcome analysis
- Real-time alerts by company or sector
